## Data Teleport Anchor
Across billions of light-years, point-to-point data transfer pins two lonely civilizations together as each other’s anchors.

Point-to-point teleportation. Costs 10 kAE.  
There are 16 color channels, and different colors are isolated from one another. Using <ItemImage id="data_energistics:data_crystal_cutting_knife" /> can ignore the color restriction.
