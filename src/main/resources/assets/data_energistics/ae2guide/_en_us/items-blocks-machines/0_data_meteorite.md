A meteorite from an unknown region of space, carrying the force to tear through dimensions.  
You do not know why it arrived here, only that it is nothing like an ordinary meteorite.  
It serves as the starting point of this mod.  
There is a 27% chance that a meteorite contains 1 to 2 Charged Data Crystal Motherrocks.
